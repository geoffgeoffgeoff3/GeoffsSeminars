Set of Support

  • ACM paper, 1965
  • Idea - minimize irrelevant inferences
  • Inspiration - reasoning over mathematical theories
  • Idea - proof by refutation, goal directness
  • Idea - disallow inferences between axioms
  • Implementation - put axioms into the processed set
  • Effect - all inferences have ancestor in negated conjecture
  • Added (incomplete) bounds on clause size and inference depth
  • Theory - put satisfiable subset into the processed set
  • Basis for model and semantic resolution
  • Motivation for the given clause algorithm

Unit and UR Resolution

  • AFIPS paper, 1964
  • Idea - Unit resolution infers shorter clauses
  • Idea - Unit sections leading to unit clauses
  • Complete for Horn clauses
  • Useful addition to complete systems

Demodulation

  • JACM paper, 1967
  • Idea - Love for mathematical problems, with equality
  • Idea - Automate Birkoff's theorem (replacing equals by equals)
  • Idea - Unit equalities, k-modulants, reducing number of symbols
  • Not well founded
  • Foundation for paramodulation
  • Basis for Knuth-Bendix completion, superposition.

Paramodulation

  • MI paper, 1969
  • Idea - Avoid adding axioms of equality
  • Idea - From clause, into clause
  • Reflexivity axioms? - Wos-Robinson conjecture
  • Various complete combinations of inference rules
  • Inspiration for superposition, and much more

Condensed Detachment

  • CADE-11 paper, 1992
  • Idea - Encode condensed detachment at the meta-level, for some rule

    (provable(rule(A,C)) & provable(A)) => provable(C)
    ~provable(rule(A,C) | ~provable(A) | provable(C)

  • Idea - Provide known axioms at the meta-level
  • Idea - Use binary resolution to apply condensed detachment
  • Example - equivalential calculus

    ~provable(e(A,C)) | ~provable(A) | provable(C) #--CD
    provable(e(e(X,Y),e(Y,X))) #--EC-4
    provable(e(e(e(X,Y),Z),e(X,e(Y,Z)))) #--EC-5
    ~provable(e(e(e(a,v),e(c,a)),e(b,c))) #--Deny EC-1

  • Proof length measured by uses of CD
