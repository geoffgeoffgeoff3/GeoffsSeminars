About Proofs

  • Proofs (and proof-like structures) are an essential part of science
  • Different proofs have different properties
    • Formal methods ... ease of verification, new algorithms
    • Planning ... alternative action sequences
    • Knowledge-based reasoning ... independent agents' views
    • Security ... different ground observations, trust
    • Mathematics ... elegance, different axiomatic bases
  • Different proofs are good proofs!

  • We combine (ATP) proofs to form different new proofs
