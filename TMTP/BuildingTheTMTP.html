Model Files

  • Divided into domains, like the TPTP and TSTP
  • Files named DDDNNNFV.MMM-SZS.m
    • DDDNNNFV is the TPTP problem name
    • MMM is the model number
    • SZS is the SZS dataform, e.g., FMo, Sat

    • Model finder information
