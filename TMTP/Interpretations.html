Interpretations and Models

What is an Interpretation?

  • Logical language ... variables, constants, functions, predicates, etc.
  • An interpretation consists of a ...
    • Domain (finite or infinite)
    • Interpretation of ground terms to domain elements
    • Interpretion of closed formulae to {true,false}
  • A model interprets given closed formulae to true

Complete, Partial, Strictly Partial

  • An interpretation is complete ... it interprets everything
  • A partial interpretation might not interpret everything
  • A strictly partial interpretation does not interpret everything
  • A strictly partial interpretation can be complete for a set

Types of Interpretations

  • Herbrand interpretations
  • Finite domain interpretations
  • Integer and Real interpretations
