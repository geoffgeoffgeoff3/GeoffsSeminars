Groups

  • 207 abstract problems, 1 FOF problem, 206 CNF problems
    • One of the original TPTP domains
    • Second largest mathematical domain in the TPTP
  • Various axiom sets
    • Five for groups
    • Axioms for semigroups, subgroups, monoids, etc.
    • Some relational formulation, in terms of product/3
    • One axiomatization with equality as a consequence [Ver92]
  • Large proportion of pure equality problems
    • Large proportion of UEQ problems
  • Good source of range restricted problems
  • Includes the previously open quasigroup problems of Bennett, solved by Fujita, Slaney, etc, e.g., GRP128-2.006.p
  • McCune's problems establishing single axioms, e.g., GRP049-1.p
