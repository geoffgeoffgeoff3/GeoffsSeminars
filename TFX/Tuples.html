Tuples

The Idea

  • Types and terms in []s
  • Can contain any type of term, heterogeneously
  • Tuples removed from FOF and plain TFF

Examples

  • [$real,$real] and [2.3,-6.6]
  • p: ([$int,$i,$o] * $o * $int) > $o
    q: ($int * $i) > $o
    me: $i
    ! [X: $int] : p([33,me,$true],! [Y: $i] : q(X,Y),27)
