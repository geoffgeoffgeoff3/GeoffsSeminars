The Problem

Youth Suicide

  • Third leading cause of death in adolescents/young adults
  • Low percentage receive services

Suicide Risk Assessment Training

  • A foundation clinical skill all therapists must learn
  • Only 50% of psychologists report receiving training
  • Consequences of poor training
    • Hospitalization
    • Discontinuation of services
    • Provider burden
  • Effective training is hard to achieve
  • Workshops are forgotten
    • Increased knowledge but not skills
    • Improvements not maintained
  • Effective trainings = intensive & expensive!
