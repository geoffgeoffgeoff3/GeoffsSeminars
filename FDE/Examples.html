  • Comments
    • #1 shows all are sublogics of FOL ... they are paraconsistent
    • If q is replaced by p in #2, it's an RM3 and FDE theorem ... a tinge of relevance
    • #3 confirms FDE is a sublogic of A3
    • #4 places RM3 apart from A3 and FDE
    • #5 and #6 show neither FDE→cmi nor FDE→con is a sublogic of the other
