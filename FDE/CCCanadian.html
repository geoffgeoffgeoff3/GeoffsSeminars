Classical Material Implication (→cmi)

  • Emphasizes the classical aspect of FDE
    • Classical implication
      φ ψ φ → ψ
      T T T
      T F F
      F T T
      F F T
    • Designated antecedent ... conditional = consequent, e.g.,
      (T → N) = N,     (B → B) = B
    • Undesignated antecedent ... conditional = T, e.g.,
      (N → N) = T,     (F → B) = T

  • FDE and other logics
    • FDE restricted to T, N, F is the logic K3
      • K3 plus →cmi is synonymous to Ł3
    • FDE restricted to T, B, F is the logic LP
      • LP plus →cmi is the logic A3
      • LP plus contrapostive of →cmi is RM3

  • Strange feature ...
    • (N ↔ F) = T
