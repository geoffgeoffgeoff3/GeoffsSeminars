TPTP Standards for Questions and Answers

Questions

  • Conjecture with outermost existentially quantified answer variables
  • Given the role question
  • E.g., 
    fof(pXY,question,   ? [X,Y] : p(X,Y)  ).

Short Answers

  • SZS answers short list_of_definite_answer_tuples
  • E.g., 
    SZS answers short for ANS001+1 [[a,b],[b,Z],[a,c]]

Long Answers

  • SZS answers start for problem_file_name
    fof(some_name,answer,
        quantification_from_question
            disjunction_of_conjunctions_of_binding_equations & the_question,
        answer_to(name_of_question,[])).
    ...
    ...
    SZS answers end for problem_file_name
  • E.g.., 
    SZS answers start for ANS001+1
fof(1,answer,
    ? [X,Y] :
      ( X = a & Y = b & p(X,Y) ),
    answer_to(pXY,[])).
fof(2,answer,
    ? [X,Y] :
      ( X = b & Y = Z & p(X,Y) ),
    answer_to(pXY,[])).
fof(3,answer,
    ? [X,Y] :
      ( X = a & Y = c & p(X,Y) ),
    answer_to(pXY,[])).
SZS answers end for ANS001+1
  • Intermediate forms possible
