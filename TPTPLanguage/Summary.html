TPTP Language Summary

A Key to Success

  • Same language for input (problems) and output (derivations, models)
    Inter-operability between systems and tools
  • Simple concrete syntax that is Prolog like
  • Easily identified Interpreted symbols

Languages

  • CNF and FOF - stable and defacto standards
  • TF0 and TH0 - stable and being used
  • TF1 and TH1 - stable, but not much use yet
  • TFA - TF0 or TF1 with arithmetic, being used
  • TFX - published and coming soon
  • TH0 and TH1 include arithmetic, but not much use yet

Language Definition in BNF

  • Extended BNF
    • <source>      ::= <general_term>
    • <source>      :== <dag_source> | <internal_source>
    • <lower_word>  ::- <lower_alpha><alpha_numeric>*
    • <lower_alpha> ::: [a-z]
  • LR1 - lex/yacc/flex/bison friendly
  • Hyperlinked online
