Commonsense Reasoning

"Reasoning methods that exhibit the features of human thinking"

Application Domains

  • Reasoning over ontologies
  • Question answering
  • Planning
  • Education
  • Personalization
  • Games (http://www.sporcle.com/games/thelastprime/flood_world_capitals)

The Nature of the Reasoning

  • Large theories (many symbols, many axioms, many theorems)
  • Human expectations (fast reasoning, friendly interfaces, world knowledge)
  • Inconsistent, uncertain, imprecise, temporal, incomplete ... (aaaargh)
  • Unnecessary axioms (how to choose the right few)
  • Short and simple arguments (easy once you have the axioms)
