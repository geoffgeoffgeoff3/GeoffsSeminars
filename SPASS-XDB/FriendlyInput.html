A Prize Winning Curie

  • Name a member of the Curie family who has won two prizes 
    fof(who,conjecture,
    ? [FullName,GivenName,Prize] :
      ( is_familyName('Curie',FullName)
      & has_WonPrize(FullName,Prize)
      & is_givenName(GivenName,FullName)
      & printonly(printall(nl,nl,GivenName,' won the ',Prize,nl)) ) ).

Attempto Controlled English (ACE)

  • A controlled (unambiguous) natural language
  • Service for converting ACE to TPTP, at University of Zurich
  • Name a person who is a member of the n:Curie-family and who wins a prize A and a prize B that is not the prize A. 
    fof(name,axiom,(
    ? [A,B,C,D,E,F,G] :
      ( person(A)
      & member(B)
      & relation(B,of,C)
      & 'Curie-family'(C)
      & A = B
      & prize(D)
      & has_part(G,D)
      & predicate2(E,win,A,G)
      & prize(F)
      & F != D
      & has_part(G,F)
      & object(G,na,countable,na,eq,2) ) )).
  • Postprocess to "natural" TPTP form (not integrated)
  • Online Access
