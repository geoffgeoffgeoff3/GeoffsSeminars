Rich Theories

What is a Rich Theory?

  • Many predicate and function symbols
  • Many axioms
  • Theorems provable from a small subset of axioms
  • Examples:
    • Set theory
    • Number theory
    • Geometry
    • Homological algebra

Techniques

  • Dieting
  • Iterative lemma replacement
  • Iterative lemma addition
  • Relevance
    • Brute force
    • Intelligence
    • Hierarchies
    • Binary selection
