The Basic Process - Example

  • Conjecture:
        C = b
  • Axioms:
        E1 = a | b
        E2 = b => a
        E3 = (~a & (b | c)) | (a & ~b & ~c)
        E4 = b | (a <=> c)

  • E1 and E2 are superfluous

  • Negated conjecture = ~b
