Approaches to Proving Hard Theorems

Peter Principle Points

  • ATP systems can be (refutation) complete, but ...
    • They require infinite resources
    • The search space is O(N 2SearchDepth)
  • Realistically, they have PPPs

Allah or the Mountain

or
