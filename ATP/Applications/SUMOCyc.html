Knowledge-based Reasoning in SUMO and Cyc

Reasoning Approach

  • Impose sort constraints
  • Tolerate incompleteness wrt ontology-specific reasoning

Application of ATP

  • IDV used to view derivations
  • Problems added to the TPTP problem library
