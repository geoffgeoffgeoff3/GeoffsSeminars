Using Lemmas

Background

  • Many problems beyond the reach of (resource limited) ATP
  • Prove and use intermediate lemmas
  • Lemmas from humans or computers

Iterative Lemma Usage

  • ILA: As lemmas are proved, add to the axioms
  • ILR: As lemmas are proved, replace the previous one
  • ILS: Remove previous lemmas that are easily proved
  • Each lemma must be provable from axioms and preceding lemmas

Recursive Lemma Usage

  • Set the conjecture as the initial target
  • Use axioms and mimimal combinations of lemmas to prove the target
  • Selection: Greedily apply recursively to prove lemmas (and use relevance)
  • Minimization: Tolerant branch-and-bound, with iterative deepening

Evaluation

  • Tested on illustrative test problems
  • Results show efficacy of the various techniques
  • High complexity and resource usage
