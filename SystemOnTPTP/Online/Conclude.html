Conclusion

Users can easily and quickly submit a problem

  • In TPTP syntax
  • To an appropriate ATP system

User absolved of responsibilities and chores

  • Selecting systems
  • Installing systems
  • Tranforming and formatting problems
  • Controlling system execution

For users focusing on problem content

  • Not for batch use
  • Not for dedicated use of a particular system

Developers are invited to submit their systems
