What is Required?

Rigorous, scientific method

  • Developers need to understand ...
    which techniques work well for what types of problems
  • Users need to know ...
    which systems are most likely to solve given problems

Evaluation of systems is a key

  • Identify specialist and general capabilities of ATP techniques
  • Provide information to potential users about systems' qualities
  • Substantiate realistic and meaningful performance claims
  • Stimulate ATP research and system development

Sadly ...

  • Complete analysis is impossible (or P = NP)
  • Developers' empirical analysis is often inadequate
