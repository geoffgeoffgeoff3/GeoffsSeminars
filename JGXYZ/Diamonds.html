The Truth and Information Diamonds

The Diamonds

  • The Truth Diamond represents the amount of truth
  • The Information Diamond represents the amount of information

Connectives

  • Negation (¬) inverts the Truth Diamond
  • Conjunction (∧) is the meet (lowest point)
  • Disjunction (∨) is the join (highest point)

Notation

  • ⟦φ⟧ = TV means that φ has the truth value TV (T or B or N or F)
