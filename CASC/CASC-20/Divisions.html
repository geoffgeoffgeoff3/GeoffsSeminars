Competition Divisions

      2 ranking classes (for analysis of cappability)

  • The FOF Division (FOF axioms with a provable conjecture)
    • 2 problem classes; 2 ranking classes

  • The SAT Division (satisfiable clause sets)
    • 2 problem classes; 2 ranking classes

  • The EPR Division (finite HU clause sets)
    • 2 problem classes (unsatisfiable and satisfiable);
      1 ranking class

  • The UEQ Division (unsatisfiable unit equality clauses)
    • 1 problem class; 1 ranking class
