Cons and Pros

Cons

  • Incremental development
  • Excessive tuning (use unseen problems)
  • Focus on implementation at cost of theory
  • Tension

Pros

  • Evaluates ATP systems
  • Tests the theory
  • Rewards implementation efforts
  • Stimulates ATP research in general
  • Stimulates research towards autonomous systems
  • Provides motivation for implementing and fixing systems
  • Provides an inspiring environment for personal interaction
  • Exposes ATP systems within and beyond the ATP community
  • Inspires others
