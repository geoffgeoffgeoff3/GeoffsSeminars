Competition Organization

Key Points

  • No more than 3 organizers
  • Homogeneous divisions, wrt known systems
  • Reasonable, agreed upon, operational rules
    • Selection of problems
    • Time limits
    • Execution control
    • Transparency
  • Panel of 3 respected researchers

The CASC Organization

  • Initially two organizers, now one organizer
  • Divisions based on SPCs
  • Design online, about 5 months in advance
  • Panel of 3, with roll-over

Incremental Change

  • Incremental changes to competition =
    incremental changes to systems
  • Carefully and gently influence research
  • Design based on science, not intuition
  • Give competitors plenty of time to adapt
