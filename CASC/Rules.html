Rules for Entry

Entry

  • Systems can be entered at only the division level
  • Systems can be entered into more than one division
  • Systems not entered assumed to perform worse

System Properties

  • Robust installation
  • Automatic and clean operation
  • No storing information for individual TPTP problems
  • Repeatable performance

Quality Assurance

  • Soundness testing before and after competition
  • Solutions checked before and after competition
  • System descriptions expose features, strategies, and implementation
  • Sources made publicly available on WWW
