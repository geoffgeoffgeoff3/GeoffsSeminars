Conclusion

Shallow vs. Deep Encoding

  • Shallow is quick-and-dirty, deep is slow-and-clean
  • Deep axioms are more reusable than shallow axioms
  • Deep axiomatization reused SUMO content
    • Properties of relations
    • Units and measures
    • Process, action, time
  • Deep axiomatizations include unnecessary axioms

Biology in SUMO

  • Contains ...
    • Standard biological taxonomy
    • Information about human organs and their function
    • A domain ontology of viruses
  • Much more can be encoded ... a great student project!

Strengths and Weaknesses

  • Formal logic - clear semantics
  • TPTP - the standard for ATP
  • Proof can be exported to explanation, e.g., P.rex
  • Logic encoding - can be too general
  • First-order logic - is not powerful enough
  • A full ontology - requires accurate selection
