The SZS Ontologies

Ontology of Results and Outputs

  • Aim
    • Correctly and precisely specify what is known/established
    • Set standard presentation

  • Motivation
    • Avoid misinterpretation of ATP output
      • Meaningless output
      • Non-standard output
      • Imprecise output
      • Misleading output
    • Support tool embedding

Features

  • Developed from a semantic basis
  • Success ontology
  • No success ontology
  • Dataform ontology
  • Standards for reporting status and output
  • Forthcoming standards for questions and answers

Full details available online from www.tptp.org/TSTP
