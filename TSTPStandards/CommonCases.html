Common Cases

Theorem

  • Every model of Ax is a model of C
    • Proof of C from Ax,
    • Refutation of Ax U {~C} or CNF(Ax U {~C})
  • Common use: FOF theorems

Satisfiable

  • Some models of Ax are models of C
    • Model
    • Saturation
  • Common use: Satisfiable sets of FOF axioms, satisfiable sets of CNF

CounterSatisfiable

  • Some models of Ax are models of ~C
    • Model
    • Saturation
  • FOF non-theorems

Unsatisfiable

  • Every interpretation is a model of Ax and ~C
    • Proof of ~F,
    • Refutation of F
  • Unsatisfiable sets of CNF
