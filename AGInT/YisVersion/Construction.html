Construction

Technique

  • Construct conjecture, prove as theorem
  • Syntactic, possibly with filtering
  • Semantic, using examples
  • Examples
    • Given axioms, enumerate terms and quantifications, conjecture relations
    • Conjecture equivalence of generated concepts with the same examples

Advantages

  • May produce more theorems if conservative
  • Easy to generate many conjectures

Disadvantages

  • Unsound: Conjecture may be disproved, may need correction
  • Syntactic conjectures probably boring
