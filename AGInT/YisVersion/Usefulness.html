Usefulness

Usefulness

  • Contributions to proofs of interesting theorems
  • Techniques
    • Watch use as parent in inferences
  • Examples
    • In set theory, 
      ! [X] : equal(union(X,X),X)
      is useful, but 
      ! [X] : equal(X,X)
      is redundant, and not often useful.

Implementation

  • Fraction of children that are interesting
  • Usefulness = NumberOfInterestingChildren / NumberOfChildren
  • High usefulness is good
