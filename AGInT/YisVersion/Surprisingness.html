Surprisingness

  • New relationships between concepts
  • Techniques
    • Look for new combinations of predicates and functions
  • Examples
    • It would be surprising if: 
      ! [X] :
  ( shoe_size(X)
 => prime(X) )

Implementation

  • Measure new co-occurences of symbols relative to axioms
  • Repetitivity (of a symbol pair) = 1 - (NumberOfAxiomsWithBoth / NumberOfAxiomsWithEither)
  • Surprisingness = Sum of repetitivities of symbol pairs in the theorem
  • High surprisingness is good
